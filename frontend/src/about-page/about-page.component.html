The Why..

Saving money can be real drag, especially for students who are juggling classes, assignments, and a social life. The traditional method of saving money is boring and uninspiring, making it hard for students to stay motivated and committed to their savings goals. As a result, many students end up blowing their money on impulse purchases and regrettable decisions, leaving them broke and stressed out. We created a fun and gamified student savings platform that will make saving money exciting and engaging for students.

The How...

SmartSave is all about a savings platform that makes saving money exciting and engaging for students. By combining innovative gamification techniques, social features, and personal finance advice, we have creates a platform that empowers students to take control of their finances and achieve their dreams without sacrificing their fun and freedom.
